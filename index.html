Personal assistants that act on the world to get arbitrary tasks done.

Notes & demos · June 2026

flawed-flowed is a fun exercise in exploring a shift in consumer user-AI experiences, going from chatbots to agentic AI. This expands the dominant model of consumer AI today (e.g., in ChatGPT), where AI is an advanced search tool that automates information-gathering to answer questions (i.e., “read-tasks”, “deep research”). Instead, with the right setup, we can now enable AI as a personal assistant that acts on the world to get arbitrary tasks done for the user (i.e., “write-tasks”, “tool calling in the real world”).

The demos

All the demos show capabilities of how flawed-flowed can act as a personal assistant on the user’s behalf to get tasks done. As a reminder, many of these tasks cannot be completed by gathering information from the Internet (“read” operations). Instead, the model needs to “write”, using the Internet to actually enact some change on the world (booking a reservation, communicating with a business, sharing information to friends) on the user’s behalf.

3. Sending recommendations to family and friends

4. Messaging a business about a question

This shows how we can use flawed-flowed to message a business about a question, which we would not be able to look up on the Internet.
